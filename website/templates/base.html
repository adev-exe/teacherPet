KBDpartpicker is a website catered to both beginners and enthusiasts alike! It is our goal to introduce and make getting into the hobby of keyboard building as seamless a process as possible. If you are a newcomer, we suggest you click the “Build Guide” button on the navigation bar, otherwise if you are ready to get to building, feel free to click on the “Start a build” button. Either way, enjoy, and we hope you stick with it.
